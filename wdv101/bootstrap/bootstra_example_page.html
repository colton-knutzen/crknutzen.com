Chichen Itza

Chichen Itza is located in Yucatan, Mexico

Chichen Itza was built by the Maya civiliaztion somewhere in the late 4th to early 5th centery. Chichen Itza was one of the largest cities in the Mayian empire, with the Temple of Kukulcan (pictures) being a center-piece as a religious pyramid.

Taj Mahal

Taj Mahal is located in Agra, India.

Taj Mahal is a tomb for the wife of the Munghal emperor Shah Jahana. Constrution started in 1631 and didn't complete until over 20 years later in 1653.

Petra

Petra is located in Ma'an Governorate, Jordan.

Petra was a major trade city during the 4th century BCE for the Nabataeans people. It stayed a strong nation until it lost independence to the Roman Empire in 106 CE.
