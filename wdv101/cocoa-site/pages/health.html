Health Facts about Cocoa

One of the largest health benefits to cocoa is it is one of the richest sources of polyphenols. Polyphenols have numerous benefits including lower blood sugar, prevent risk of heart disease and blood clots, and protect against cancer.

Cocoa is also helpful with mental health! It is a natural anti-depressant that help boost the neurotransmitters that release dopamine, serotonin, and endorphins. We've also associated the taste of chocolate to happy times since childhood, which helps relax the mind.

Unfortunately in our highly-processed foods society, a lot of the pure, natural minerals and vitamins get cooked out when processed. So as with everything, moderation is key to true health.
