Welcome to Chayot Chronicles

Chayot Chronicles is a webnovel series following the adventures of Chayots, people blessed with extordianary abilities by the Chachika. Together, they make up the Enochi Order, a secret society that has existed since the dawn of humanity. Chayots help mankind grow and protect them from dangers they've only heard of in myths and legends.
