Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Chayot Chronicles?

Chayot Chronicles is a webnovel series following the adventures of Chayots, people blessed by the Chachika with extordianary abilities. Together, they make up the Enochi Order, a secret society that has existed since the dawn of humanity. Chayots help mankind grow and prosper, and protect them from dangers only heard of in myths and legends.

How is the Chayot Chronicles differnt than a normal novel?

The only difference is that it is published online, only at chayotchronicles.com, rather than in traditional paper and cover form. This format gives you assesiblity to the stories of the Enochi Order anywhere there is internet connection, and gives us the space to dive deeper into this world.

What else does this site provide?

Chayot Chronicles is also a database. Here you'll find pages that expand upon characters, the cultures, locations, and lore. This gives you as readers easy access to everything you many need to know, and we can share more about the stories of the Chayot world that just couldn't be fit into the plot.

How often are updates?

We try to publish at least one new articles daily, whether that be a new page about a business, expanding upon a characters' past that was reveled in the recent chapter, or teasing for something in the future.
Chapters are published once a week. During this time we will be working on the next novel in the series. There will be an "off-season" when we are putting the final touches on the next novel.
